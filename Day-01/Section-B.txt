11. Explain CIA Triad with real-life examples
Confidentiality:
Protecting sensitive data from unauthorized access.
Example: Password-protected bank accounts.
Integrity:
Ensuring data remains accurate and unaltered.
Example: Hash verification of downloaded software.
Availability:
Ensuring systems and services are accessible when needed.
Example: Backup servers during system failure.


12. Differentiate between Threat, Vulnerability, and Risk
Term	Explanation	Example
Threat	Potential danger	Hacker
Vulnerability	Weakness	Weak password
Risk	Impact if exploited	Account compromise


13. Why are cybersecurity frameworks important?
Frameworks provide a standardized approach to identifying, managing, and reducing cybersecurity risks. They help organizations follow best practices, ensure compliance, and improve security posture.


14. Explain NIST NICE Framework
The NIST NICE Framework categorizes cybersecurity roles, skills, and tasks. It helps individuals understand career paths and helps organizations define cybersecurity job roles clearly.


15. How does CVSS help organizations?
CVSS helps organizations prioritize vulnerabilities by severity, allowing them to focus on fixing the most critical issues first.
